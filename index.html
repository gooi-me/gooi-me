Organic waste recycling made easy

Gooi makes composting your food waste as easy as throwing it in the bin.

Our goal is to divert organic waste from landfills and ensure it nourishes the earth instead.

WHAT WE DO

Gooi offers weekly collection of food scraps from your doorstep.

Ready to join the food waste revolution?

We coordinate food-waste delivery to composters around Cape Town.

Interested in receiving some food scraps for you home composting system?



WHY WE DO IT

All food used to be returned to earth as compost. There was a time before landfills and municipal rubbish trucks. Food was grown close by, every scrap was used, and the remains nourished the soil for future life.

the story

Moving back to this cycle is not a far-off idea, all we need is coordination and some willing gooiers and groeiers.

how we compost

We do it because we love to celebrate life.

Say goodbye to the hassle of disposing of your organic waste and embrace effortless sustainability with gooi.
